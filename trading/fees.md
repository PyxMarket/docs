# Fees

## Trading Fees

| | Fee |
|---|---|
| **Maker** | -1 bps (rebate) |
| **Taker** | 7 bps |

Makers receive a rebate for providing liquidity. Takers pay a fee for removing liquidity.

## Fee Distribution

- **70%** goes to the market creator
- **30%** goes to the protocol as staking rewards

## Gas Fees

Each transaction costs a flat 1 cent USDC as gas. This applies to all on-chain actions including placing orders, cancellations, and redemptions. Gas fees go to validators as block rewards.

## Bridge Fees

No fees are charged for deposits or withdrawals.
