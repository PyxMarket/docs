# Get Testnet USDC

Since PyxMarket is on testnet, you need test tokens to trade. The faucet gives you free test USDC.

## Steps

1. Connect your wallet
2. Click the **Faucet** button in the header
3. You'll receive test ETH (for gas) and 10,000 USDC on Arbitrum Sepolia
4. Use the **Deposit** button to bridge USDC from Arbitrum to PyxChain

## Notes

- The faucet sends tokens on **Arbitrum Sepolia** (L1 testnet)
- You must bridge USDC to PyxChain before you can trade
- Faucet tokens have no real value
