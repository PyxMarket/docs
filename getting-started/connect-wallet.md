# Connect Wallet

PyxMarket uses [Privy](https://www.privy.io/) for wallet connection, supporting both embedded wallets and external wallets like MetaMask.

## Steps

1. Go to [pyxmarket.com](https://pyxmarket.com)
2. Click **Connect** in the top right
3. Choose your login method:
   - **Email** — creates an embedded wallet automatically
   - **MetaMask** or other browser wallet
4. Once connected, your wallet address and USDC balance appear in the header

## Network

PyxMarket runs on PyxChain (Chain ID: 1681). Your wallet will be prompted to add the network automatically when needed.
